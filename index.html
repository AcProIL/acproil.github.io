Introduction

Peano’s Interlingua (also known as Latino sine Flexione: latin without inflexions) is a language constructed by Giuseppe Peano at the beginning of the last century. This simplified Latin is designed to be a instrument for international cooperation, firstly in the academic sphere.

After a short grammar study, everyone can learn to compose in this language using only an Interlingua (or Latin) dictionary.
